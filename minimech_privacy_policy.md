# Privacy Policy

**Effective date: July 18, 2026**

This Privacy Policy applies to the MiniMech mobile application (the “Application”), developed and operated by ReWire LLC.

## Information Collection and Use

MiniMech does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information or other user data.

The Application does not use user accounts, advertising services, analytics services, tracking technologies, or cloud-based data storage. Gameplay data and application settings, if any, are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to ReWire LLC or third parties.

## Device Permissions

MiniMech does not request access to personal information or sensitive device features for the purpose of collecting user data.

## Third-Party Services

MiniMech does not include third-party services that collect or transmit user data on behalf of ReWire LLC.

The Application may be downloaded through third-party application stores, such as the Apple App Store or Google Play. Those services may independently collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies. ReWire LLC does not control the data collected by application-store operators.

## Communications

If you contact ReWire LLC by email, we will receive your email address and any information you voluntarily include in your message. This information will be used only to respond to your inquiry and for related recordkeeping or legal purposes. It will not be sold or used for unrelated marketing.

You may request deletion of correspondence containing your personal information by contacting us at [david+minimech@rewire.llc](mailto:david+minimech@rewire.llc), subject to any applicable legal retention requirements.

## Children’s Privacy

MiniMech does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information to ReWire LLC through an email or other direct communication, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

## Security

Because MiniMech does not collect or transmit user data, no user information is stored on servers operated by ReWire LLC. Information voluntarily submitted through email is handled using reasonable safeguards, although no method of electronic communication or storage is completely secure.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

ReWire LLC may update this Privacy Policy if the Application’s features or data practices change. The updated policy will be posted with a revised effective date.

If a future version of MiniMech begins collecting user data, this Privacy Policy and the applicable app-store privacy disclosures will be updated before or when that collection begins.
